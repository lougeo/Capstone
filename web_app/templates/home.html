Welcome to GoodScriptBadScript


This is made for your amusement!

Have some fun testing out your favorite movie!


This is a tool which models any movie script (or other text) you want, and tells you how good it thinks the script will do as a movie.

It makes a predicition based on how good it thinks the script will do:
- On IMDb
- On Rotten Tomatoes
- At the Box Office (Measured by worldwide gross revenue / budget)
